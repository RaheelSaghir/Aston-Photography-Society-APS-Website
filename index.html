About Aston Photography Society

The Aston Photography Society (APS) was originally created by a small group of friends with aspirations of photography. This aspiration was later founded into the current APS to bring together photographers of all levels at Aston University. Our aim is to help members grow creatively and technically, share their work, and capture the moments that tell countless stories.
